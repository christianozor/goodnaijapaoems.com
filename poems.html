"MY MOTHER BY BISI OLUGBOYEGA

It is pleasant to be born
It is important to live
It is compulsory to be fed
It is worthy to be bred,
It is fortunate to have a mother.

I couldn't have been conceived
I couldn't have been born,
I couldn't have been bred,
But for my mother.

In her bosom, she cuddled me
Sleeping or wailing she cuddled me
With soothing palm, she caressed me
Healthy or ailing, she caressed me
Cuddling, caressing depict her love.

She taught me laughter when i cried,
She taught me love when i frowned,
She taught me tottering when i crawled,
She taught me babbling when i hooted,
Ser teaching last in my life.

Laughter celebrates happiness,
Compassion celebrates love,
Tottering made me walk uprightly,
Babbling made me walk rightly,
I learn your lessons permanently.

Mama Sweet Mama,
Sweeter than honey,
Prettier than gold,
More precious than diamond.

Mother, oh precious mother!
Your loving spirit thrills me,
Your milk softens my heart,
Priceless mother, how wonderful you are.
